Walk-in Resources Near You

No appointment needed — help is already here.

📍 Kota, Rajasthan

Annapurna Rasoi — Govt. Subsidised Canteen

Food

Hot meals at just ₹5–₹8 per plate. Multiple locations across Kota city, run by the Rajasthan government for those in need.

🕐 Daily, morning & evening ₹5 per meal
🏠

Urban Shelter Home (Rein Basera), Kota

Shelter

Night shelter run by Kota Municipal Corporation. Free beds, blankets, and basic amenities for homeless individuals. Open daily dusk to dawn.

🌙 Dusk to dawn daily Free
🏥

New Medical College Hospital (NMCH), Kota

Medical

Government hospital with free OPD, emergency care, and medicines under Ayushman Bharat. Located on Rangbari Road, Kota.

📍 Rangbari Road, Kota 🕐 24/7 Emergency Free OPD
👕

Sewa Bharati Kota

Clothing

NGO distributing clothes, blankets, and essential supplies. Especially active in winter drives across Kota.

📞 Contact Sewa Bharati office, Kota Free
⚖️

District Legal Services Authority (DLSA), Kota

Legal Aid

Free legal aid for underprivileged citizens. Located at the District Court Complex, Kota.

📍 District Court Complex, Kota 🕐 Mon–Sat, 10am–5pm Free
📞

Rajasthan Social Welfare Helplines

Helpline

Call 181 (Women Helpline) or 1800-180-6127 (Social Justice Dept.) for referrals to shelters, food, and welfare schemes anywhere in Rajasthan.

🕐 24/7, Free to call Free Call
